Privacy Policy for NoSweat AI Extension

Effective Date: April 27, 2025

NoSweat AI ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it through this Privacy Policy. This policy describes the types of information we may collect when you use our Chrome Extension and how we use, store, and protect that information.

Information We Collect

  • Authentication Information: When you create an account or log in, we collect your email address to authenticate you.
  • Usage Information: We track your monthly usage (e.g., the number of questions solved) to enforce plan limits (free or paid).
  • Screenshot Data: When you request a solve, a screenshot of your active browser tab is captured and sent securely to our server for analysis.

We do not collect or store:

  • Your browsing history
  • Your passwords
  • Payment information (payments are handled securely by Stripe)

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and manage your account.
  • To track your usage and apply monthly limits based on your subscription plan.
  • To analyze screenshots you choose to send to us and return an answer.
  • To manage subscription billing and status through Stripe.

Data Storage and Security

  • Authentication data (email) and usage metrics are securely stored in our database (Firebase).
  • Screenshots are processed immediately and not stored after processing.
  • We implement strong security measures to protect your data against unauthorized access.

Third-Party Services

We rely on trusted third parties:

  • Firebase for authentication and user data storage.
  • Stripe for subscription management and billing.

These services are governed by their own privacy policies.

User Control

You can request to delete your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at nosweatai@gmail.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the extension and on our website, and your continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
